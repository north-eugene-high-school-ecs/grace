Major Characters

Arrietty is the main character. Some of the main aspects of her personality is her bravery and stubborness.

Sho (Shawn in the English dub) is the human who befriends Arrietty. In the movie, he's staying with his Great Aunt.

Haru (Miss Hara in the English dub) is the housekeeper of Sho's Great Aunt's house. She is the antagonist of the movie.
